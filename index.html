Brief Intro

Hi, my name is Braedon. I'm a recent college grad, but am always learning new things. My major was Family History, with a minor in Front-End Design. I've always been interested in creating digital content and bringing ideas to life.

My interest in front-end design was actually sparked by Adobe Flash. Yes, the same Adobe Flash that will no longer be supported as 2020. This awoke a passion for creating cartoons and games. My skills include web-development (especially HTML, CSS, and Javascript), SQLite (e.g., relational databases), and basic graphic design.
